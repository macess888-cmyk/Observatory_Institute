# 5. ADVERSARIAL FINDING AN-001 — USGS SOLAR AND GEOMAGNETIC EARTHQUAKE NULL POSITION

    Finding ID:
    AN-001

    Source:
    United States Geological Survey

    Source type:
    OFFICIAL SCIENTIFIC ASSESSMENT

    Status:
    OFFICIAL NULL FINDING

    Domains:
    SOLAR
    GEOMAGNETIC
    SEISMIC

## 5.1 Core finding

The USGS reports that no demonstrated causal relationship has been established between:

    solar flares
    geomagnetic storms
    space weather
    earthquake occurrence

The approximately eleven-year solar cycle does not correspond to a comparable global earthquake cycle.

## 5.2 Relevance

This directly challenges claims that ordinary variation in:

    sunspot activity
    solar flares
    geomagnetic disturbance
    solar-cycle phase

provides a reliable earthquake-triggering signal.

## 5.3 Limits of the finding

The finding does not prove:

    all weak triggering mechanisms are impossible
    no fault can ever respond to an external perturbation
    no electromagnetic observation can accompany an earthquake

It establishes that a reliable general causal relationship has not been demonstrated.

## 5.4 Observatory OS disposition

    STATUS:
    OFFICIAL NULL FINDING

    EFFECT ON SUPPORTING CORPUS:
    SOLAR-SEISMIC CLAIM REMAINS CONTESTED

    GENERAL TRIGGERING CLAIM:
    NOT ESTABLISHED
