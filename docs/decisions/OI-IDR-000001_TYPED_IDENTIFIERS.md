# OI-IDR-000001

# Institutional Decision Record

## Title

Adoption of Typed Institutional Identifiers

---

## Status

Accepted

---

## Date

2026-07-13

---

## Decision

The Observatory Institute adopts typed identifiers for all institutional artifacts.

Examples include:

OI-CON

OI-CHA

OI-PRO

OI-RES

OI-ONT

OI-STD

OI-IDR

OI-PUB

OI-PLA

OI-OFF

OI-GRA

---

## Context

As the Institute grows, sequential numbering alone becomes insufficient to communicate the purpose and classification of institutional artifacts.

Typed identifiers improve readability, discoverability, long-term stewardship, and institutional scalability.

---

## Alternatives Considered

Sequential numbering without document types.

This approach was rejected because artifact purpose would not be immediately apparent.

---

## Consequences

Institutional artifacts become self-describing.

Repository organization becomes more scalable.

Cross-references become easier to understand.

Future contributors can immediately identify artifact purpose.

---

## Steward

Founding Stewards

---

## Review

No review currently required.

This decision becomes part of the institutional architecture.
